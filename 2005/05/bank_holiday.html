BANK HOLIDAY

So, the May Bank Holiday is finally over, my head is still throbbing, my eyesight has returned to hazy and it's back to work. Highlights of the weekend; Friday night in Hotels then to Dragon Bar for beer, Saturday at Encompass (purely to escape the rain), Emily and Julie's Extra Extraordinary Jumble Sale for relief and scotch, then the rave of raves secretsundaze till the fat lady sang, and believe me by Tuesday she had. Shouts out to all my ravers!

The question remains, why not one Bank Holiday a month?
