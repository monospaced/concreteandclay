CLASSIC TOAST

Ingredients:

Bread (white or wheat)
butter or margarine
jam or jelly

Procedure:

Place 1 slice bread in toaster. Push lever down. When toast begins to rise, carefully remove from toaster. Lightly whip butter. Spread favorite toppings with broad, even knife strokes.

Makes 1 serving.
