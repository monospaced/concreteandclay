GO!ZILLA SOSHO

Terry Hart (Drama, Cuckoo Couture) and James Priestley (secretsundaze, All Over My Face) present Go!Zilla, this Saturday 5th November at Sosho, 2 Tabernacle St, London EC2. 9pm - 4am.

This saturday sees Go!Zilla teaming up with one of Italy's finest and longest running electronic record labels, Nature Records, as they play host to Francisco's "Music Business" Album Launch Party. Joining them and responsible for the disco in the basement bar at Sosho are local talents Johnny Hiller from Laser Magnetic and Fonteyn from Computer Blue.
