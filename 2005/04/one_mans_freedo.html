ONE MAN'S FREEDOM FIGHTER, ANOTHER MAN'S... TEACHER

It's not everyday that you met a genuine, 100% freedom fighter (some have called him a terrorist), let alone allow him loose with a pack of inquisitive 14 year olds. The struggle against Apartheid is something that your average joe can't even begin to imagine as they walk through the leafy suburbs or even on our own "frontlines". And yet there are those amongst us who have fought the true fight against oppression and have dodged the bullets to tell the tales. It was a privilige to be in the same room as this quiet, reserved gentle man told his story.
