MEEUW CHRISTMAS PARTY

Now that London is covered with a thick layer of toxic black smoke, lets go to Liege. The best winter festaval in my eyes takes place in Belgium. With Felix Cubin, Jason Forrest (my very favourite Donna Summer), Bodenstandig 2000, Gangpol und Mit, and many more playing live with an arsenal of DJs on the stand-by, this will be a night to remember. Jason Forrest has been one of the most experimental producers in the last ten years. His "I know you are going to like this" CDR was has been an urban legend since I got sent it years back. Make your way to Les Caves de Cornillons-Liege. December the 17th 2005.
