BLOW OFF THE COBWEBS

Come to The Tate Britain 5th of August, on the banks of the Thames overlooking MI5 and your sure for a big suprise. Suitable Case For Treatment playing insano country blues gypsy metal live on the front lawn.

The night is a launch for their debut album "Of Motets and Misdirection" released on The Thin Man. Other bands include White Night, a new wave indie party band from Glasgow who are playing their only gig here before they split up on Saturday night, Two Men In A Boat and The Mabuses, who formerly recorded on Rough Trade. Beer and BBQ is available to all, plus free/late entry into the gallery.

Suitable Case come highly recommended by 60's agit rock legends The MC5 and their single "Wooga" has been touted as anthem of the week in Kerrang! Find it on iTunes along with all other Thin Man releases; Milk and Beef and Box Codax.

As you may have guessed I am The Thin Man, so you kids get your kooky asses down there, get lean but make sure you're up in time to hear White Night play their first and last radio session on the DeXter Bentley Show, Resonance FM the next day 12-2 it's the last time you'll evr hear them!
