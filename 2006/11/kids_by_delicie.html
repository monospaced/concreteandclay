Kids
Magistrates dished me out my asbo
Tagged me for pushing haribo
Well patchoulied up like a grebo
Better bone structure than Gretta Garbo
Herbie 2 got the flow
Unrestricted like a beat pipeline from Tajikstan to
Idaho
Yo
What's up kids, Drinks are on me
Poppers whizz and sensi
Wicked lyrical workshops going for free
Beat seekers allowance has hooked me
Poem for the free
Just sign on every week cos they melted down your key
Tattooed your bike lock code on your foreskin just in case she can't read
That's if with your blairite clubfoot you'll get to plant your seed
So can you read?
With Bill Oddie watching Jeckyll from my hide
I step forth onto the high street and see my lighter side
The blue sunrise sets on me as all shackled I descend
Into the tenebers of
Babylon
cos codes I broke and do bend
The moral of the story is and shall go down in history
Meet me with the kids and
Blyth
and instigate our mutiny.
