GOOGOLPLEX

  1. The number 1 followed by a googol zeros
  2. A feeling of intense paranoia and/or self-loathing prompted by searching for your own name, or that of a friend on the ubiquitous search engine of roughly the same name, "that screen's given me a right googolplex"

Discovered in Stepney Green late in the night of the 25th of April after searching for a friend's name in the google search engine in their presence and that of many other friends. The only listed entry came up on the screen at PervWatch.org. Though the friend assured collected other friends that the entry was nothing to do with him, the mirth that followed, bracketed by uncomfortable silences, rendered the situation memorable for the wrong reasons. The friend in question remarked "I wouldn't have minded if it was pervwatch.com, but .org is a government organisation."
