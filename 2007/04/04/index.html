FROZEN ELEPHANTS

Please welcome the latest and overall sixth release by the label and net-project Frozenelephantsmusic.com.

Connected Dots Connected Ideas features a collection of 13 unique tracks by international sound-artists from Japan, the United States, France, the UK, Sweden and Germany. The compilation gathers various free improvised works and digital compositions, connecting swift moments of intuition with characteristics of jazz, minimalism and electro-acoustics.
