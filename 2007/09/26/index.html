EFTERKLANG...

Efterklang will release Parades, their second full-length album, on October 15th. It's released on The Leaf Label worldwide and on Rumraket in Denmark. They'll also be touring, for the first time in two years, from October the 16th, passing through France, Spain, Sweden, Norway, Denmark, England, Scotland, N. Ireland, Ireland, Belgium and The Netherlands; full tour info at www.efterklang.net.

NEW CAGEY HOUSE...

Cagey House have just released a new EP called Model City on Canadian net-label Nishi. It's their third release on Nisih and we think it's the best one yet. A noir vibe with lots of deep reverb and fuzzy tones, strange, colorful noise and clangy percussion. All the tracks are free to download or stream here http://www.archive.org/details/nsh117 and here's a taster:
