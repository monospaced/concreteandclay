AVATAR ONE...

Madukara has been making music for 13 years, he takes regular singing lessons with a professional Opera singer and has a degree in music after completing an intensive three year course at the London Music School in Wapping. He often recieves intvites to play as guest gutairist with Kulashaker and Finley Brown for gigs and recording sessions. AVATAR ONE is his two year project and has so far recorded over fifty tracks and three albums worth of material. He is currently working on his fourth coming album.
