SAMPLESWAP

After seven years of steady growth in the underground electronic music scene, SampleSwap.org has officially launched a public website featuring 4.6 GB of free professional quality loops and audio samples. Drum kits from Roland, Emu and Linn are among the highlights on this easy to use site.
